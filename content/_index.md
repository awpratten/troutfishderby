Trout Fish Derby is the post experimental collaboration of guitarist Warren Pratten and electronic musician Alex Schmoll. The two first crossed paths as members of the Magic Daffodils and quickly discovered a strong musical rapport. 


The sound has been described as a sonic ride that lives somewhere between experimental spontaneity, and hypnotica.  The vibe is chill, vivid and dreamlike.  Using loops, drones, programmed beats, found sounds, prepared tapes, and spoken word, they layer, sculpt, and improvise to shape each performance.
